Event: The Funky Branch Lagos Last Edition Was a Mega Hit!!! Celebrities, Music, and Fashion!!!

January 24, 2020 Naijablog Admin Entertainment 0

The Christmas edition of one of Nigeria’s most anticipated parties – The Funky Brunch Lagos turned out an incredible and impressive massive crowd of upwardly mobile and luxury lifestyle lovers with over 3000 guests across Nigeria with a high concentration of celebrities, millennials, upwardly mobile gentlemen and women in entertainment, corporate, art and government from within and outside Nigeria in attendance. 

Guests were treated to an exquisitely decorated welcome area entrance which had them taking hundreds of selfies, delicious feasts for the stomach, endless drinks, crazy laughter, great energy, good vibes, and the most beautiful car show display with luxury cars from Ferrari’s to Rolls Royce’s.

The Christmas edition was hosted by one of the continent’s biggest exports- D’Banj. Some other celebrities in attendance included Netflix hit series Dynasty’s lead act- Sam Adegoke, Burna Boy, Naira Marley, Zlatan Ibile, Patoranking, Mercy Eke, YCee, Rahman Jago, Timini, and a host of many other A-list celebrities,  VVIPs, Captains and Leader’s from the lifestyle industry. 

“D’Banj hosting the Funky Brunch Christmas edition was a match made in lifestyle heaven for me because he ticks all the green buttons of lifestyle and undiluted enjoyment which are part of his brand anecdotes. And that marries well into the Funky Brunch vision coupled with celebrations excellence in entertainment. So bringing them all together with D’Banj leading the park is absolute bliss for us as producers”- Efe Tommy(Founder, The Funky Brunch).

The party which took place on Monday, December 23, 2019, at Balmoral Hall, Victoria Island, Lagos, was marked with style and grandeur which led to guest tagging it “The Party of the Year”. It featured an array of the best Nigerian DJ line up such as DJ Consequence, DJ Obi, DJ Prince, DJ K Flow, DJ Six7even, DJ Blink, DJ Last Don, LatstheDJ, DJ FD, DJ Paranormal, DJ Harmony and 3ple DJ, who took turns to serenade guests with the best music with great support from three of the best hypemen in the country-MC Fish, MC AK Smuth, Jerry Shaffer and Jimmie all signed to Moet Hennessy, keeping guests on their toes from start to finish.

“Just like our goal to create experiences that span from Lagos to Miami and Ibiza to Monaco during sunset, the enjoyment don’t stop. It’s why it gets really critical scouting for DJs and setting up a great line up”- Sep Nimi Briggs(Co-Founder: The Funky Brunch)

The Christmas edition of The Funky Brunch wad powered by Bespoke PR, a leading Boutique agency, small in size, but, of course, loud in its messaging. Founded in 2016 by Efe Tommy. 

“We are a full-service marketing communications agency with a core focus within the fashion, lifestyle and entertainment sectors across all facets of marketing and public relations, from brand strategy and media outreach to events, sponsorships, and VIP relations.”- Efe Tommy(Founder).

And supported by Veuve Cliquot, SoundCity and Los Angeles based mobile calling app, ESDIAC global app whose aim is to deliver connectivity and entertain celebrations. 

“The partnership between The Funky Brunch and ESDIAC Global App, enhances alternative connectivity, not only in Nigeria but worldwide, They believe in having a positive impact on communities they serve. We look forward to this partnership and the opportunity to have our community connect with their friends and family beyond Lagos.”- Efe Tommy(owner of BespokePR & The Funky Brunch. 

The Funky Brunch Lagos has become the definitive experience for fashion, food, and music in Nigeria for the imaginative millennial. Unrivaled fashionable guests, celebrities and the ambiance combine for the most electrifying party in Africa.
