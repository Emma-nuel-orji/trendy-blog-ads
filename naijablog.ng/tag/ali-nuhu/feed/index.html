]]> Actor, Ali Nuhu has called on Muslim no to do something that will drive others people away from the religion.

The award winning actor says this in his special Friday message share on his Facebook page.

While emphasizing how Islamic religion welcome others, he charges Muslim to stop condemning people because of the littles things, because it drives them aways.

His words read; “Make things easy for others. Give glad tidings and don’t chase people away from the religion. Islam welcomes all and we shouldn’t be so harsh on others that we end up condemning and judging them for small things that drive them away from Islam. Don’t be too strict or harsh in your manners or opinions that you intimidate others. Islam is the middle way and one of peace so be a person who is good to others and is an example of Islam in every way. Jumua Mubarak“

The post #endnorthbanditry, #endbokoharam: Ali Nuhu Beg Arewa Leaders to Stop Boko Haram and bandits, say it destroy Arewa region appeared first on Naijablog.

]]> Following the #ENDSARS protests that erupted across Nigeria few days Now, Kannywood award Winning actor Ali Nuhu has has instigated new tags, #endnorthbanditry and #endbokoharam.

Ali Nuhu who share the tags on his official Facebook Page that have over 1 Million followers, call on Arewa rich men and women, Government, and leaders to end the incessant killing by Boko Haram and Bandits in the regions.

He said in Hausa “Kira gare mu Talakawan AREWA da Malaman mu, da Sarakunan mu, da Attajiran mu, da Jami’an Gwamnatin mu, mu hada karfi Wuri guda don ganin an kawo karshen wannan masifa da ta addabi yankin mu na AREWA.  Allah ka karfafe mu, Allah kai mana dauki #endnorthbanditry #endbokoharam”

Translate to English “Call on us, the people of AREWA, our teachers, our rulers, our rich people, and our government officials to work together to end this scourge that is plaguing our region.  May God strengthen us, may God take us  # endnorthbanditry #endbokoharam”
