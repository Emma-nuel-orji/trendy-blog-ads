]]> Nigerian comedian, basket mouth speak on how he feel when they accused wizkid of being proud.

In his explanation,

“All these talks about Wizkid being proud will always be funny to me. 2014 I had my show in Lagos and I really needed Wizkid to perform and I was willing to pay him anything.

I contacted him and he told not to bother about paying him. He promised to come as a surprise guest & put up a show for free.

3 days to the event, Wizkid called me that he won’t be able to show up coz a very important family emergency had happened. I was really heartbroken but I just had to accept the reality. 35 minutes after that call, Wizkid asked me to contact any Nigerian artiste I will like to book for the event and get back to him with the bills. I quickly contacted Lil Kesh, Kcee, Yemi Alade and Phyno. The bill was about 40 Million Naira and Wizkid cleared it all. Mannn! I thought I was dreaming. Wizkid is not the guy social media keeps trying to make y’all believe he is” ~ Basket Mouth.

]]> Nigerian Comedian, BasketMouth has react to the killing of David, sound engineer Lynch to death by Hausa Okada boys in Lekki phase 1 in Lagos State.

In a post share on his Facebook page, Basket Mouth said:

I only read & hear about unbelievable and terrifying incidents like this and now it has hit so close to home.

David was a dedicated member of my church. He was innocent and did not deserve the horror he was dealt by this crazy mob that night. A father of two young children, a husband & friend to many and he got beaten & lynched in Lekki Phase 1. Lekki phase 1 is usually littered with several check points. It’s unbelievable that terror is right at our doorstep.

Thanks to efforts by many via social media & news media. We have seen statements from authorities but much more needs to be done not only to bring #JusticeForDave but also to ensure this menacing wave of jungle justice, comes to a stop.
