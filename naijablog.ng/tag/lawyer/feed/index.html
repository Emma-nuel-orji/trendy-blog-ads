]]> The police command in Akwa Ibom has arrested Ekere Ebong, a lawyer who was seen in a viral video brutalizing his wife in Uyo, the state capital.

It would be recalled that in a video that made the rounds on social media over the weekend, Ebong was seen beating his wife at their Uyo residence. In the five-minute clip, the woman can be seen lying on the ground with blood coming out of her mouth, while begging her husband to stop the beating.

The woman was rescued by her neighbours, who challenged Ebong.One of the neighbours said the man has been beating his wife almost every night in the last four years. The video elicited angry reactions on social media, with most Nigerians calling on the police to arrest the lawyer.

In a statement released on Monday, Odiko Macdon, Akwa Ibom police spokesperson, said the lawyer is “a known habitual perpetrator of violent acts against his wife over the years”. Macdon said the lawyer will be arraigned in court after the conclusion of investigation.

“As an agency saddled with the protection of lives and property and maintenance of law and order, we cannot allow Barrister Ebong to continue this flagrant abuse of human rights, hence, the commissioner of police, CP Olatoye Durosinmi ordered the arrest of the suspect to prevent murder and breakdown of law and order,” the police spokesperson said.

The answer is that lawyer are not burried face down, and they are not burried 7fts instead of 6fts.

The believe that lawyer are buried differently from others people, is just a myth, not real.

If lawyer who is a Muslim died, he is burried according to Muslim right.
