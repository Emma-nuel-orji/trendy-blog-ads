]]> World Record holder in Women’s 100m Hurdels, Tobi Amusan has kicked off 2024 with yet another milestone.

Amusan raced to the finish line in 7.77s to win the Women 60m Hurdles event at the World Athletics Indoor Tour Gold in Astana.

Her time becomes becomes her new Personal Best and African Record breaking Glory Alozie’s time of 7.82s that was set 25 years ago in Madrid.
